The general responsibilities of an Area Leader are as follows:

Note: The duties of an Area Leader can be shared among individuals but one person needs to be designated as an Area Leader in each Area for efficient communication and coordination.
  1. Act as liaison with the CBC Compiler on data matters and the CBC Coordinator on all other CBC matters.
  2. Become acquainted with the data collection requirements of the CBC and the associated forms and documents for data compilation and submission.
  3. Coordinate the activities of the Survey Team for the Area, providing guidance, direction, and support as required.
  4. With the Survey Team, determine how the data are to be collected (paper forms, eBird, combination, etc.).
  5. Ensure the group waiver form is completed prior to the start of the surveying.
  6. Allocate the effort of the Survey Team across the CBC Area being surveyed and ensure, to the extent possible given weather and other conditions, the entire Area is surveyed.
  7. Compile all the data obtained for the Area surveyed, submit to the Compiler, and be available to answer questions the Compiler may have or to provide additional information if required.
